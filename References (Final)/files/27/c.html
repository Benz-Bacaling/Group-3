Datasets

Chemistry and Chemical & Biological Engineering Datasets

Chemical structure and related data repositories. Be sure to check the individual resources for their data reuse policies and licensing terms before using the data in your research.

  • PubChem
    PubChem Substance, Compound, and BioAssay Data. There are over 300 million substances, 100 million compound, and almost 300 million bioactivites. Data is available in SDF, CSV, and more.
  • ChEMBL
    ChEMBL data focused on small molecules and related bioactivity data. There are over 2 million compound records, 1 million assays, and 11,000 targets. Data is available as SDF, SQL, and more.
  • ChEBI
    ChEBI is a dictionary of small chemical compound molecular entities. SDF and ontology files are available for download.
  • GDB Databases
    Largest collection of virtual molecules.
  • EPA CompTox
    A variety of chemical data related to properties, toxicology, environment, and more is available.
  • ZINC 15 Database
    ZINC15 is a database of commercially available compounds.
  • Crystallography Open Database
    Over 350,000 crystal structure files of organic, inorganic, and metal organic compounds.

Repositories

Chemistry and Chemical & Biological Engineering Repositories

  • Re3data
    re3data is the Registry of Research Data Repositories. It is a useful resource for finding appropriate data repositories to discover and share chemical data.
  • Figshare
    A general purpose repository.
